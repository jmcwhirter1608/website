Applications Engineering

In all forms, I enjoy finding new ways to use new technology to improve processes that may seem unrelated. For example, Drones have been a very useful product for the photography and videography industries. But, did you know that it has also been useful for agriculture? During past summers, I have learned to use a DJI Phantom 4 Pro to map crop circles using Climate FieldView and DroneDeploy software. With this additive information, we are better capable to maintain crops and predict yield.
