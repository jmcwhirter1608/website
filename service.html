Service

Chi Omega Sorority has hosted a dance competition which supports many philanthropies at Texas A&M University for 43 years. Through Aggie Men's Club, I devoted 6 hours a week to learn a group dance in order to earn money for Kairos Christian School. Unfortunately, our group did not place at the event, but we still raised nearly $10000.

Since 2005, Aggie Men's club and Maggies teamed up to host a 5 km "fun-run" to raise money and awareness for Big Brothers and Sisters of Brazos Valley and Kairos Christian school. Through Aggie Men's club, I am currently raising awareness and selling tickets for this event which can be found by clicking the image above.

Kairos Christian school was founded in Guatemala for the purpose of providing education and nutrition to the native children. Since 2010, it has grown to support grades 1 through 8. Through Aggie Men's club, I support this organization and help fundraise through various activities like Songfest, Glow 5k, and more.

The Reach Project is an organization that provides for the "Invisible Aggies" at Bryan/College Station, TX. Through Aggie Men's club, I have worked with them to provide meal distribution Monday afternoon and service a free Trunk or Treat event for Invisible Aggies. Invisible Aggies is a term we use to describe beneficial members to Texas A&M that are often overlooked by the community. The goal is to give back to this aggies by providing resources and encouragement.

The Big Event is a one day student-run service project for the Bryan/College Station community. It is the goal of the event to provide back by volunteering to help residents for a few hours with a job requested by the resident. I will be participating for the first time this year.

In my semesters at South Plains College, I grew close to the Baptist student ministry. I joined the Student Leadership team in Fall 2018 to provide for the community by servicing 50 cent lunches, hosting student worship nights, and setting up and striking events planned by the Baptist Student Ministry. In addition, I helped spread the gospel to students and furthered my relationship with God.
